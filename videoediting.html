Video Editing

My first experience with video editing was when I was probably around 7 years of age on the legendary Windows Movie Maker. A lot of people actually started editing like that. From Movie Maker I moved to Camtasia Studio which was quite popular back in the days. After some experience with Camtasia I remember moving on to Sony Vegas Pro where I would make compilations of my highlights in video games such as Counter-Strike. But that couldn't be taken as serious video editing. In 2017-2018 I fell in love with video editing again, as my high school needed students that are capable of video editing - this was my time to get back into editing, and since then I've been learning it on and off when I have free time.
